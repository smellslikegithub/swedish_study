# En and Ett in Swedish

## Overview

Swedish nouns have two grammatical genders:

* en-words (common gender)
* ett-words (neuter gender)

Examples:

* en lägenhet
* en stol
* ett hus
* ett rum

The gender affects:

* articles
* adjective endings
* definite forms

---

# Why gender matters

Using the correct gender changes the form of the noun.

| Indefinite | Definite |
| ---------- | -------- |
| en stol    | stolen   |
| ett hus    | huset    |
| en bok     | boken    |
| ett rum    | rummet   |

Incorrect gender sounds unnatural to native speakers.

---

# Important statistics

* About 74% of Swedish nouns are en-words
* About 26% are ett-words

If unsure, guessing "en" is statistically safer.

---

# Useful patterns

## 1. Words ending in -a

Most nouns ending in -a are en-words.

Examples:

* en lampa
* en vecka
* en kamera

Exceptions exist.

---

## 2. Memorization is important

There are patterns, but many exceptions.

Best practice:

* memorize the article with the noun

Do:

* en stol
* ett bord
* en lägenhet
* ett sovrum

Not:

* stol
* bord
* lägenhet
* sovrum

Treat the article as part of the word.

# Adjective agreement

| Gender   | Example        |
| -------- | -------------- |
| en-word  | en brun stol   |
| ett-word | ett brunt bord |
| plural   | bruna stolar   |

| ------------- | ---------------- |

# Definite noun forms

| Indefinite  | Definite  |
| ----------- | --------- |
| en gardin   | gardinen  |
| ett fönster | fönstret  |
| ett sovrum  | sovrummet |

Plural definite:

* gardinerna
* fönstren
* sovrummen

---

# Useful grammar notes

* båda + definite plural

  * båda sovrummen
  * båda fönstren

* modal verbs + infinitive

  * kan använda
  * vill köpa
  * måste vänta

* subordinate clauses

  * när jag hörde ...
  * att Max kommer tillbaka ...

* V2 word order

  * På rasterna lyssnar jag ibland på musik.

* possessives

  * din lägenhet
  * ditt rum

---

# Study tips

When learning vocabulary, memorize:

1. article
2. noun
3. definite form
4. plural form

Example:

* ett fönster
* fönstret
* fönster
* fönstren

This makes Swedish grammar easier long-term.
